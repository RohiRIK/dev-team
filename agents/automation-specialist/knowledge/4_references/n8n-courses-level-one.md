83	Level one: Introduction#
84	
85	Welcome to the n8n Course Level 1!
86	
87	Is this course right for me?#
88	
89	This course introduces you to the fundamental concepts within n8n and develops your low-code automation expertise.
90	
91	This course is for you if you:
92	
93	Are starting to use n8n for the first time.
94	Are looking for some extra help creating your first workflow.
95	Want to automate processes in your personal or working life.
96	
97	This course introduces n8n concepts and demonstrates practical workflow building without assuming any prior familiarity with n8n. If you'd like to get a feel for the basics without as much explanation, consult our quickstart guide.
98	
99	What will I learn in this course?#
100	
101	We believe in learning by doing. You can expect some theoretical information about the basic concepts and components of n8n, followed by practice of building workflows step by step.
102	
103	By the end of this course you will know:
104	
105	How to set up n8n and navigate the Editor UI.
106	How n8n structures data.
107	How to configure different node parameters and add credentials.
108	When and how to use conditional logic in workflows.
109	How to schedule and control workflows.
110	How to import, download, and share workflows with others.
111	
112	You will build two workflows:
113	
114	A two-node workflow to get articles from Hacker News
115	A seven-node workflow to help your client get records from a data warehouse, filter them, make calculations, and notify team members about the results
116	What do I need to get started?#
117	n8n set up: You can use n8n Cloud (or the self-hosted version if you have experience hosting services).
118	A course user ID: Sign up here to get your unique ID and other credentials you will need in this course (Level 1).
119	Basic knowledge of JavaScript and APIs would be helpful, but isn't necessary.
120	An account on the n8n community forum if you wish to receive a profile badge and avatar upon successful completion.
121	How long does the course take?#
122	
123	Completing the course should take around two hours. You don't have to complete it in one go; feel free to take breaks and resume whenever you are ready.
124	
125	How do I complete the course?#
126	
127	There are two milestones in this course that test your knowledge of what you have learned in the lessons:
128	
129	Building the main workflow
130	Passing the quiz at the end of the course
131	
132	Check your progress
133	
134	You can always check your progress throughout the course by entering your unique ID here.
135	
136	If you complete the milestones above, you will get a badge and an avatar in your forum profile. You can then share your profile and course verification ID to showcase your n8n skills to others.
