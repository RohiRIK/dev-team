## Content and Tone

*   **Tone:** Professional, concise, and action-oriented.
*   **Profile Summary:** A brief, powerful summary of your expertise and experience.
*   **Skills:** Categorize skills for clarity (e.g., Cloud Platforms, Automation & Scripting).
*   **Work Experience:** Use strong action verbs to describe your accomplishments. Quantify achievements whenever possible.
*   **Notable Projects:** This is a key section to highlight specific accomplishments within a role.
