# Action Verbs for Resumes

This document contains a categorized list of strong action verbs. The `cv-generator` agent should use these verbs to start each bullet point in the "Professional Experience" section of the resume, as instructed in `resume_best_practices.md`.

---

## The Goal: Show, Don't Tell

Using strong, specific action verbs helps to demonstrate your impact and accomplishments, rather than just listing your responsibilities. This is a key part of crafting a resume that stands out to recruiters.

---

## Verb Categories

### For Building & Creating

- Architected
- Built
- Created
- Designed
- Developed
- Engineered
- Implemented
- Launched
- Pioneered
- Programmed

### For Improving & Optimizing

- Accelerated
- Automated
- Enhanced
- Improved
- Optimized
- Overhauled
- Refactored
- Re-engineered
- Reduced
- Streamlined
- Upgraded

### For Leading & Collaborating

- Advocated
- Collaborated
- Coordinated
- Guided
- Led
- Managed
- Mentored
- Spearheaded

### For Analyzing & Maintaining

- Analyzed
- Assessed
- Debugged
- Diagnosed
- Documented
- Maintained
- Monitored
- Resolved
- Supported
- Tested
- Troubleshot
