# Knowledge Creation

Knowledge files are the primary source of information for an agent. They should be well-structured, comprehensive, and easy to understand. The following are some guidelines for creating effective knowledge files:

-   **Create at least 15 knowledge documents:** To ensure that the agent is knowledgeable, you should create at least 15 knowledge documents for it.
-   **Include best practices and cheat sheets:** In addition to the core knowledge documents, you should also create best practices and cheat sheets to help the agent to perform its tasks more effectively.
-   **Use Markdown:** Knowledge files should be written in Markdown.
-   **Be Concise:** Keep the content of the knowledge files concise and to the point.
-   **Use Headings:** Use headings to structure the content of the knowledge files.
-   **Provide Examples:** Provide examples to illustrate the concepts.
-   **Keep it Updated:** Keep the knowledge files up-to-date with the latest information.
